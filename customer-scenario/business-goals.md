# Business Goals

The customer wants to modernize its technology environment to support faster operations, stronger governance, and production-ready AI adoption.

## Primary Business Goals

1. Improve operational efficiency by reducing manual workflows.
2. Modernize legacy data and application platforms.
3. Establish a secure Azure foundation for AI workloads.
4. Improve visibility into application health, usage, and business performance.
5. Enable internal knowledge search using approved documents and data sources.
6. Strengthen security, governance, and compliance practices.
7. Create a phased roadmap that can be implemented without disrupting operations.

## Desired Customer Outcomes

The modernization effort should help the organization achieve:

- Faster access to internal knowledge
- More reliable reporting and analytics
- Reduced manual handoffs
- Stronger auditability
- Improved system resiliency
- Better visibility into cost and performance
- A secure foundation for AI-enabled workflows

## Success Criteria

The engagement will be considered successful if the customer has:

- A documented current-state assessment
- A target-state Azure architecture
- A prioritized modernization roadmap
- Identified blockers and mitigation strategies
- Security and governance recommendations
- A production-readiness checklist for AI workloads
- Clear success metrics for adoption and business value
